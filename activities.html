Our Initiatives

Our aim is two fold, first to train the youth and then to empower them to serve the society.

ENDURO: Youth Skill Development

Training programs designed to equip young individuals with essential technical and soft skills, enabling them to become confident and employable.

Community Service Drives

Regular social service activities including cleanliness drives, food distribution, plantation efforts, and support for essential community needs.

The Vedic Gurukul: IKS Initiative

Classes to introduce students to the rich knowledge of Indian Vedas and to teach moral values based on the Indian scriptures.

Mental Wellness Sessions

Providing assistance, mentorship, and counseling to children from underserved backgrounds.

Leadership & Personality Development

Structured programs that help youth build confidence, communication skills, leadership traits, and a service-oriented mindset.
