Shipping Policy

Last updated: October 6, 2025

Overview

EmpowerNext Foundation is a registered non-profit organization. We primarily accept donations and do not sell or ship physical products.

Digital delivery (receipts & acknowledgments)

All donation acknowledgments, tax receipts, and confirmation letters are sent digitally to the donor's registered email address. These are typically delivered within 2-5 working days of a successful transaction.

Physical delivery (rare / exceptional)

In the rare instance that a physical item (for example: printed acknowledgement, receipt, or merchandise) needs to be dispatched, EmpowerNext Foundation will ship the item to the postal address provided by the donor. Physical items are dispatched within 7-10 working days from the date the shipment is confirmed.

Donors will be notified by email with tracking information where applicable.
